Robson Ranch OB Rule

Out of Bounds (OB)

Goal: Avoid "The Walk of Shame" and speed up play.

Penalty: 2 Strokes.

Procedure: If you hit your ball Out of Bounds, you do NOT need to return to the tee. Instead, place your ball in the fairway adjacent to where it went out of bounds.

(This simulates hitting a 3rd shot from the tee and landing it in the fairway. You are now lying 3, hitting your 4th shot).

Local Rule: The "Lost but Found" Option

To be used when a ball is lost but agreed by the group NOT to be Out of Bounds.

Purpose: To speed up play when a ball is clearly in play but cannot be located (e.g., buried in deep rough or leaves).

"If a player‚Äôs ball is lost and the group agrees it is not Out of Bounds, the player may opt to treat the lost ball as an Unplayable Lie rather than taking Stroke and Distance."

  • Agreement: The group must agree on the estimated vicinity where the ball was lost.
  • Penalty: One (1) Stroke.
  • Procedure: Drop within two club-lengths of the estimated spot (no closer to the hole).
  • Result: You lie 2 (shot + penalty) and are hitting your 3rd shot.

Note: If the group believes the ball may be Out of Bounds, this rule does NOT apply. You must proceed under standard OB rules (2 stroke penalty, drop in fairway).
